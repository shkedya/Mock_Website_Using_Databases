Welcome to the Mart!

From Kanto to the Galar region, Our Poké Mart handles hundreds of sales from dozens of trainers a year, all at different stages in their pokemon journey. Our database is used by many trainers to record their sales and can allow a trainer to see how much they spend on items in the Mart, how much they need to save, and how many of the 8 Gym Badges are required in order to actually purchase the items.Our Poké Mart also allows Trainers to trade their pokemon with other trainers. The database will keep a record of each trade so that users can look up when they made certain trades and to whom.
